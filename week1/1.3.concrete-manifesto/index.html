Stanley Brouwn: A Short Manifesto

When science and art are entirely melted together to something new

When the people will have lost their remembrance and thus will have no past, only future.

When they will have to discover everything every moment again and again

When they will have lost their need for contact with others

Then they will live in a world of only colour, light, space, time, sounds and movement

Then colour light space time sounds and movement will be free

No music

No theatre

No art

There will be

Sound

Colour

Light

Space

Time

Movement
